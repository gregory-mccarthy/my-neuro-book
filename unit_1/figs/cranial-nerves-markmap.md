# The 12 Cranial Nerves

## Telencephalon
### CN I: Olfactory
#### Afferent
- Special Visceral: Olfaction (sense of smell)
#### Efferent
- (None)

## Diencephalon
### CN II: Optic
#### Afferent
- Special Somatic: Vision
#### Efferent
- (None)

## Midbrain (Mesencephalon)
### CN III: Oculomotor
#### Afferent
- (None)
#### Efferent
- Somatic Motor: Superior, inferior, & medial rectus; inferior oblique; levator palpebrae superioris
- Visceral Motor (Parasympathetic): Sphincter pupillae (pupil constriction), ciliary muscle (accommodation)

### CN IV: Trochlear
#### Afferent
- (None)
#### Efferent
- Somatic Motor: Superior oblique muscle

## Pons (Metencephalon)
### CN V: Trigeminal
#### Afferent
- General Somatic: Sensation from face, scalp, cornea, nasal/oral cavities, and cranial dura mater
#### Efferent
- Branchial Motor: Muscles of mastication, mylohyoid, anterior belly of digastric, tensor tympani, tensor veli palatini

### CN VI: Abducens
#### Afferent
- (None)
#### Efferent
- Somatic Motor: Lateral rectus muscle

### CN VII: Facial
#### Afferent
- Special Visceral: Taste from anterior 2/3 of tongue
- General Somatic: Sensation from a small area of the external ear
#### Efferent
- Branchial Motor: Muscles of facial expression, posterior belly of digastric, stylohyoid, stapedius
- Visceral Motor (Parasympathetic): Lacrimal, submandibular, and sublingual glands; mucous glands of nose and palate

### CN VIII: Vestibulocochlear
#### Afferent
- Special Somatic (Vestibular): Equilibrium and motion (head position)
- Special Somatic (Cochlear): Hearing
#### Efferent
- (None - primarily afferent)

## Medulla Oblongata (Myelencephalon)
### CN IX: Glossopharyngeal
#### Afferent
- Special Visceral: Taste from posterior 1/3 of tongue
- General Visceral: Sensation from carotid body/sinus, middle ear, and pharynx
- General Somatic: Sensation from the external ear
#### Efferent
- Branchial Motor: Stylopharyngeus muscle
- Visceral Motor (Parasympathetic): Parotid gland

### CN X: Vagus
#### Afferent
- General Visceral: Sensation from neck, thorax, and abdominal viscera
- Special Visceral: Taste from the epiglottis and palate
- General Somatic: Sensation from external ear and posterior cranial fossa dura
#### Efferent
- Branchial Motor: Muscles of the pharynx, larynx, and soft palate
- Visceral Motor (Parasympathetic): Smooth muscle and glands of the pharynx, larynx, thoracic viscera, and abdominal viscera (up to the distal 1/3 of the transverse colon)

### CN XI: Accessory
#### Afferent
- (None)
#### Efferent
- Branchial Motor: Sternocleidomastoid and trapezius muscles

### CN XII: Hypoglossal
#### Afferent
- (None)
#### Efferent
- Somatic Motor: Intrinsic and extrinsic muscles of the tongue (except palatoglossus)
